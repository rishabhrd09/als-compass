Common questions about ALS caregiving and respiratory support

When does a PALS need BiPAP support & what are the signs?

Key Point: PALS have weakened diaphragm muscles → they can't exhale properly → CO₂ gets trapped inside the body.

Why plain oxygen alone is risky: Oxygen may show good O₂ levels on monitor, but internally CO₂ keeps building up silently. This is dangerous. BiPAP helps push out the CO₂ while also helping breathe in.

Signs that BiPAP is needed:

  • Morning headaches (CO₂ buildup sign)
  • Feeling tired even after sleeping
  • Breathlessness while lying down
  • Gasping or disturbed sleep
  • Daytime drowsiness/confusion
  • SpO₂ dropping below 90%

Comparison: Oxygen Only vs BiPAP

Aspect Oxygen Only BiPAP
O₂ levels Improves Improves
CO₂ removal No help Helps exhale
Safe for PALS Risky alone Recommended

Approximate Costs (India):

Recommendation: If you see these signs, consult your doctor about starting BiPAP early. Don't wait!
