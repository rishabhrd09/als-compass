Key Recommendations

  • Ensure the room has proper ventilation to maintain air quality.
  • Arrange and test all equipment at regular intervals to ensure everything is functional.
  • TV, Alexa for music, and AC are vital for mental well-being of pals
  • Set up a CCTV camera in the room and give access to family members
