Understanding how to calculate and plan backup power for medical equipment

Understanding Power Backup

Your power backup system works like a water tank. The batteries store energy (measured in Amp-hours, or Ah), the devices consume energy (measured in Watts), and the UPS converts and delivers that stored energy to your equipment.

To calculate backup time, you need to know: (1) how much energy your batteries hold, and (2) how much power your devices consume. The formula is:

Backup Time (hours) = (Battery Capacity in Wh Ã— 0.75) Ã· Total Load in Watts

We use 0.75 (75% efficiency) to account for battery and UPS energy losses
